FAQ's

What is House Party By United?

House Party by United is an exciting platform that brings the thrill of a house party to a whole new level. It's your ultimate destination to discover and purchase tickets for exclusive house parties or list your place to be hosted by passionate individuals. Get ready to immerse yourself in an unforgettable experience, connecting with like-minded party-goers and enjoying the pulsating energy of intimate gatherings. House Party by United is where unforgettable memories are made.

How do I create an event ?

Click on the button that says "host a party" or contact us on +91 8591260648 Once you navigate yourself to the page, Fill out the required information about your event, and any additional details or instructions. Choose the appropriate event category or genre to help potential attendees find your event. Submit the event for review, and publish it. Once your event is created, it should be listed on the House Party By United website, and interested users can purchase tickets to attend.

Can I invite guests through the house party website?

If you are hosting a party you will be able to review profiles of people who buy the tickets to the party and accordingly you can make your own guest list

How can I manage RSVPs and track attendance on the house party website?

Verified Profiles of guests who rsvp or show interest at an event will be shared with the host and accordingly you can manage your guestlist for the party

How can I communicate with my guests?

Once the final guestlist is shared, you can contact them via whatsapp or call.

Can I customize privacy settings for my house party event on the website?

You can contact us on +91 8591260648 if you have queries related to privacy of your event

Can I add 2 or more people along with my list or do I need to do it separately?

Yes You can book tickets for 2 or more people for a given event.

Can I order my drinks directly to the location?

You can use the Cheers by united app to order your favourite drinks at the venue

Can I access the house party website on mobile devices?

Yes the version is available on all iOS and android devices.

Is there a cost associated with using the house party website?

You have to buy a ticket and reserve your seat at a party.

How can I get in touch with the support team of the house party website?

You can always reach out to us on +91 8591260648 or mail us at Cheers@housepartybyunited.com.

How can I share updates or changes with my guests?

Once you host a party or list a place, You can coordinate with the support team to manage any changes or share updates regarding your event

Is the house party website secure and private for my event?

Yes. We at houseparty ensure safety and security of all our users.

Is it necessary to BYOB?
